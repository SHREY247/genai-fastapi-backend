ACME CORP — NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
==========================================

Welcome to Acme Corp! This guide walks you through everything you need to
complete during your first two weeks.

PRE-DAY-ONE CHECKLIST
----------------------
Before your first day, please complete the following:
1. Sign and return your offer letter and employment agreement via DocuSign.
2. Complete the background check authorization form.
3. Submit your banking details for direct deposit via the HR Portal.
4. Review the Employee Handbook (sent to your personal email).
5. Choose your laptop preference (MacBook Pro or Dell XPS) via the IT
   Equipment Request form.

DAY 1 — ORIENTATION
---------------------
Your first day begins at 10:00 AM. Here is your schedule:

10:00 - 10:30  Welcome session with your HR Business Partner
               - Overview of company culture and values
               - Review of Code of Conduct
               - Office tour (badge access, restrooms, pantry, emergency exits)

10:30 - 11:00  IT Setup
               - Receive your company laptop
               - Set up your company email (yourname@acmecorp.com)
               - Create your password (must meet the 14-character security policy)
               - Enroll in Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
               - Install required software: Slack, Zoom, 1Password, VPN client
               - Connect to the corporate Wi-Fi network

11:00 - 11:30  Access Provisioning
               - Your manager will request access to relevant systems:
                 * Jira for project tracking
                 * Confluence for documentation
                 * GitHub for source code (engineering roles)
                 * Salesforce (sales and support roles)
                 * Internal dashboards as needed
               - Access is granted based on your role following the principle
                 of least privilege

11:30 - 12:00  Benefits Enrollment Overview
               - HR will walk you through medical, dental, and vision plans
               - 401(k) enrollment and employer match details
               - Employee Stock Purchase Plan (ESPP) overview

12:00 - 1:00   Team Lunch
               - Your manager will organize a welcome lunch with the team

1:00 - 2:00    Manager 1:1
               - Introduction to team structure and current projects
               - Discussion of your role, expectations, and 30-60-90 day plan
               - Assignment of your onboarding buddy

2:00 - 3:00    Mandatory Training — Session 1
               - Information Security Awareness (online module, ~45 min)
               - Quiz must be completed with a score of 80% or higher

3:00 - 4:00    Workspace Setup
               - Customize your desk/workspace
               - Set up your development environment (engineering roles)
               - Explore the internal knowledge base on Confluence
               - Review your team's Slack channels and join relevant ones

DAY 2 — DEEP DIVE
-------------------
9:00 - 10:00   Mandatory Training — Session 2
               - Data Privacy and GDPR Compliance (online module, ~40 min)
               - Anti-Harassment and Inclusion Training (~30 min)

10:00 - 11:00  Product Overview
               - Session with the Product Manager to understand our products
               - Demo of the main customer-facing application
               - Overview of the product roadmap

11:00 - 12:00  Engineering Onboarding (Technical Roles)
               - Repository walkthrough and branching strategy
               - CI/CD pipeline overview
               - Code review process and standards
               - Local development environment verification

12:00 - 1:00   Lunch

1:00 - 2:30    Role-Specific Training
               - Engineering: architecture deep-dive and coding standards
               - Sales: CRM walkthrough and sales playbook review
               - Support: ticketing system training and SLA guidelines
               - Marketing: brand guidelines and content management system

2:30 - 3:30    HR Policies Review
               - Leave policy and PTO accrual
               - Expense reimbursement process (submit via Expense Portal)
               - Performance review cycle (June and December)
               - The travel reimbursement policy can be found in the
                 reimbursement policy document

3:30 - 4:00    End-of-Day Check-in with Manager
               - Questions and concerns
               - Confirm Week 1 priorities

DAYS 3-5 — INTEGRATION
------------------------
- Begin working on your first assigned task or project.
- Shadow a team member on at least one customer call or sprint ceremony.
- Complete any remaining mandatory training modules.
- Schedule introductory 1:1 meetings with key stakeholders:
  * Your skip-level manager
  * Cross-functional partners
  * Your onboarding buddy for weekly catch-ups
- Read the team's documentation and recent project summaries.
- Attend your first team standup and sprint planning meeting.

WEEK 2 — GETTING PRODUCTIVE
-----------------------------
- Submit your first code review or deliverable (role-dependent).
- Attend at least one company all-hands or brown-bag session.
- Complete the "Acme Culture and Values" e-learning module.
- Fill out the Week 1 onboarding feedback survey (sent via email).
- Have your first formal 1:1 with your manager to discuss:
  * Initial impressions and challenges
  * Resource needs
  * Adjusted 30-60-90 day goals

TIPS FOR SUCCESS
-----------------
- Don't hesitate to ask questions — everyone was new once!
- Keep a notebook for the first two weeks to capture key learnings.
- Leverage your onboarding buddy for informal guidance.
- Attend social events and team outings to build relationships.
- Review the company wiki regularly as it is updated frequently.

We're excited to have you on the team!
