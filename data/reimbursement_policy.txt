ACME CORP — EMPLOYEE REIMBURSEMENT POLICY
==========================================

EFFECTIVE DATE: January 1, 2025

OVERVIEW
--------
This document outlines the detailed reimbursement procedures for all employees
of Acme Corp. All business-related expenses must be pre-approved and documented
as described below. The goal of this policy is to ensure fair, transparent, and
timely reimbursement while protecting the company from unnecessary costs.

TRAVEL REIMBURSEMENT
---------------------
1. Airfare
   - Economy class is the standard for domestic flights.
   - Business class is allowed for international flights exceeding 6 hours
     with prior VP-level approval.
   - Tickets should be booked at least 14 days in advance when possible
     to secure lower fares.
   - Loyalty program benefits (miles, upgrades) accrued during business
     travel belong to the employee.

2. Hotel Accommodations
   - Domestic hotel stays are reimbursed up to $200 per night.
   - International hotel stays are reimbursed up to $300 per night.
   - Employees must use preferred hotel partners when available.
   - Extended stays exceeding 5 nights require finance team approval.
   - Room service is covered only for meals, not for laundry or
     entertainment items.

3. Ground Transportation
   - Ride-sharing services (Uber, Lyft) are preferred over rental cars
     for short trips.
   - Rental cars require manager approval and are limited to mid-size
     or compact categories.
   - Fuel for rental cars is reimbursable with receipts.
   - Parking fees at airports and hotels are reimbursable.
   - Toll charges incurred during business travel are reimbursable.

MEAL REIMBURSEMENT
-------------------
- Daily meal limits:
  - Domestic travel: $75 per day
  - International travel: $100 per day
- Meal limits cover breakfast, lunch, dinner, and reasonable snacks.
- Alcoholic beverages are NOT reimbursable under any circumstances.
- Tips up to 20% are included within the daily meal limit.
- Client meals require pre-approval from the department head and must
  include the client name, company, and business purpose on the receipt.

EQUIPMENT AND SUPPLIES
-----------------------
- Employees may be reimbursed for work-related equipment purchased with
  prior manager approval.
- Approved items include:
  - External monitors (up to $400)
  - Keyboards, mice, and other peripherals (up to $150 total)
  - Ergonomic chairs for home office (up to $500 with HR approval)
  - Noise-canceling headphones (up to $250)
- Software subscriptions must be approved by IT before purchase.
- Personal phone and internet charges are reimbursable up to $75 per
  month for employees who use personal devices for work.

PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT
--------------------------
- Conference registration fees are reimbursable with manager approval.
- Travel to conferences follows the standard travel policy above.
- Online course and certification fees are reimbursable up to $2,000 per
  calendar year through the Learning & Development program.
- Books and learning materials up to $200 per year do not require
  pre-approval.

SUBMISSION AND APPROVAL PROCESS
---------------------------------
1. All expense reports must be submitted within 30 days of the expense.
2. Receipts are required for any single expense exceeding $25.
3. Expense reports are submitted through the internal Expense Portal.
4. Approvals follow this chain:
   - Expenses under $500: direct manager approval
   - Expenses $500-$2,000: manager + finance team approval
   - Expenses over $2,000: manager + finance + VP approval
5. Approved reimbursements are paid via direct deposit within 10 business
   days of approval.
6. Rejected claims will include a reason and may be resubmitted with
   additional documentation.

POLICY VIOLATIONS
------------------
- Submitting fraudulent or inflated expense reports is grounds for
  immediate termination.
- Repeated late submissions may result in delayed processing and
  a formal warning.
- Non-compliance with pre-approval requirements may result in denial
  of the reimbursement claim.
