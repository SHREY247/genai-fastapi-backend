ACME SMARTDEVICE X1 — PRODUCT MANUAL
======================================

VERSION: 2.0 | MODEL: SDX1-2025

CHAPTER 1: INTRODUCTION
-------------------------
Congratulations on purchasing the Acme SmartDevice X1! This manual will help
you set up, use, and maintain your device. The SmartDevice X1 is a
next-generation IoT device designed for smart home automation, environmental
monitoring, and voice-assisted control. It features a 5-inch HD touchscreen,
dual-band Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.2, a built-in temperature and humidity sensor,
and a far-field microphone array for voice commands.

Package Contents:
- 1x Acme SmartDevice X1
- 1x USB-C power adapter (20W)
- 1x Quick Start Guide
- 1x Mounting bracket and screws
- 1x Warranty card

CHAPTER 2: INITIAL SETUP
--------------------------
Step 1: Unbox the device and connect the USB-C power adapter.
Step 2: The device will power on automatically and display the welcome screen.
Step 3: Select your preferred language from the list.
Step 4: Connect to your Wi-Fi network:
        - Tap Settings > Network > Wi-Fi
        - Select your network and enter the password
        - The device will confirm connection with a green checkmark
Step 5: Create your Acme Account or sign in:
        - Download the Acme Home app on your phone (iOS/Android)
        - Open the app, tap "Add Device," and follow the pairing instructions
        - The app will discover the SmartDevice X1 via Bluetooth
Step 6: Complete the firmware update when prompted. This may take 5-10 minutes.
Step 7: Customize your home screen by adding widgets for weather, calendar,
        reminders, and smart home controls.

CHAPTER 3: FEATURES AND USAGE
-------------------------------
3.1 Voice Assistant
The SmartDevice X1 includes a built-in voice assistant. Activate it by saying
"Hey Acme" followed by your command. Supported commands include:
- "Hey Acme, what's the weather today?"
- "Hey Acme, set a timer for 10 minutes."
- "Hey Acme, turn off the living room lights."
- "Hey Acme, play my morning playlist."
The voice assistant supports English, Spanish, French, and German.

3.2 Smart Home Integration
The device is compatible with major smart home ecosystems:
- Acme Home ecosystem (native)
- Google Home
- Amazon Alexa
- Apple HomeKit (via bridge)
To connect smart devices, navigate to Settings > Smart Home > Add Device.

3.3 Environmental Monitoring
The built-in sensors measure:
- Temperature (range: -10C to 50C, accuracy: +/- 0.5C)
- Humidity (range: 10% to 90%, accuracy: +/- 3%)
- Air Quality Index (using the optional AQ sensor dongle, sold separately)
Readings are displayed on the home screen and logged hourly in the Acme Home
app for trend analysis.

3.4 Display and Widgets
The 5-inch IPS display runs at 1280x720 resolution. You can customize the
home screen with up to 8 widgets:
- Clock (analog or digital)
- Weather
- Calendar (syncs with Google Calendar and Outlook)
- Photo frame (displays photos from your Acme Cloud album)
- Smart home controls
- Reminders and to-do lists

3.5 Media Playback
The device has a built-in 10W speaker with passive bass radiator. It supports:
- Streaming from Spotify, Apple Music, and Acme Music
- Bluetooth audio from your phone or tablet
- Podcast playback via the Acme Podcasts app
- Multi-room audio when paired with other Acme speakers

CHAPTER 4: TROUBLESHOOTING
----------------------------
This section covers common issues and their solutions.

4.1 Device Not Powering On
- Ensure the USB-C cable is firmly connected to both the device and the
  power adapter.
- Try a different power outlet.
- Use only the included 20W power adapter. Third-party adapters below 15W
  may not provide sufficient power.
- If the device still does not power on, perform a hardware reset by
  inserting a paperclip into the reset pinhole on the back of the device
  and holding for 10 seconds.

4.2 Wi-Fi Connectivity Issues
- Ensure your router is broadcasting on 2.4 GHz or 5 GHz (the device
  supports both).
- Move the device closer to your router to improve signal strength.
- Restart your router and the SmartDevice X1.
- Forget the network in Settings > Network > Wi-Fi > Saved Networks, then
  reconnect.
- If using a mesh network, ensure all nodes are updated to the latest
  firmware.

4.3 Overheating
The SmartDevice X1 is designed to operate in ambient temperatures between
10C and 35C. If the device overheats:
- The screen will display a thermal warning icon (thermometer with
  exclamation mark).
- The device will automatically reduce screen brightness and disable
  intensive background processes.
- Move the device away from direct sunlight, heat sources, or enclosed
  spaces with poor ventilation.
- Ensure the ventilation grilles on the back of the device are not blocked.
- Remove any cases or covers that may trap heat.
- If the device does not cool down within 15 minutes, disconnect the
  power adapter and let it rest for 30 minutes.
- If overheating persists after following all steps, contact Acme Support
  as a hardware defect may be present.
- Note: Operating the device while charging and streaming simultaneously
  in high ambient temperatures may trigger thermal throttling.

4.4 Battery and Power Issues
The SmartDevice X1 contains a 4000 mAh backup battery for power outage
continuity.
- Battery life in backup mode: approximately 3 hours with display on,
  6 hours with display off.
- To check battery health: Settings > System > Battery > Health Report.
- If the battery drains unusually fast:
  * Reduce screen brightness
  * Disable unused features (Bluetooth, voice assistant)
  * Check for background apps consuming power in Settings > Apps > Battery
    Usage
  * Perform a firmware update, as battery optimization fixes are included
    in recent releases
- The battery is not user-replaceable. If the battery holds less than 50%
  of its original capacity, contact Acme Support for a battery replacement
  service ($49 fee outside warranty).
- Do NOT attempt to open the device casing. Doing so will void the warranty.

4.5 Factory Reset
If you need to erase all data and return the device to factory settings:
- Method 1 (Software): Settings > System > Reset > Factory Reset > Confirm
- Method 2 (Hardware): Power off the device. Hold the Volume Down + Power
  buttons simultaneously for 15 seconds until the Acme logo appears.
  Select "Factory Reset" from the recovery menu using the volume buttons
  and confirm with the power button.
WARNING: A factory reset will delete all personal data, accounts, smart
home configurations, and custom settings. This action cannot be undone.
Back up your data via the Acme Home app before proceeding.

4.6 Voice Assistant Not Responding
- Ensure the microphone is not muted (check the physical mute switch on
  the top of the device).
- Check that voice assistant is enabled: Settings > Voice > Assistant > On.
- Verify the device has an active internet connection.
- Retrain the voice model: Settings > Voice > Retrain Voice > follow prompts.
- If the issue persists, perform a software reset: Settings > System >
  Restart.

CHAPTER 5: MAINTENANCE AND CARE
---------------------------------
5.1 Cleaning
- Use a soft, lint-free microfiber cloth to clean the screen.
- Do not use chemicals, abrasive cleaners, or solvents.
- The ventilation grilles can be cleaned with compressed air every 3 months
  to prevent dust buildup.

5.2 Firmware Updates
- The device checks for firmware updates automatically once per week.
- Manual check: Settings > System > Firmware > Check for Updates.
- Updates are downloaded in the background and installed during idle periods
  or at the scheduled update time (default: 3:00 AM).
- Major updates may require a device restart. A notification will appear
  24 hours before the scheduled restart.
- Do not disconnect power during a firmware update.

5.3 Storage Management
- The device has 16 GB of internal storage.
- To check available storage: Settings > System > Storage.
- Free up space by deleting unused apps, clearing cache, and transferring
  photos to Acme Cloud.
- The device does not support external storage (SD card).

5.4 Yearly Maintenance Checklist
- Replace the USB-C cable if frayed or damaged.
- Clean ventilation grilles.
- Review and remove unused smart home device pairings.
- Update the emergency contact information in the Acme Home app.
- Run a battery health check and consider replacement if below 50%.
